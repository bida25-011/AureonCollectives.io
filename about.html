About Aureon Collective

Aureon Collective was founded with a mission to bring premium smartphones to Botswana at fair prices. Starting in Gaborone, we have grown into a trusted retailer known for quality stock and friendly service.

We source authentic Apple and Samsung devices, provide local support, and ensure secure delivery across Botswana.

Our Story

Aureon Collective was founded with a mission to bring premium smartphones to Botswana at fair prices. Starting in Gaborone, we have grown into a trusted retailer known for quality stock and friendly service.
